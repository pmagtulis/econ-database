About the site

This website compiles economic statistics from government agencies. Data are collated into one file and updated regularly. Charts are automatically generated each update. The goal of this site is to be a one-stop-shop for government data. Each chart directs to a separate page containing the data.


Gross domestic product

Annual growth of Philippine economy in real terms...so far.

State revenues

Collections by the Bureaus of Internal Revenue, Customs, tax effort, revenue effort, etc.

Spending

Expenditures to GDP or the "expenditure effort" of the government...so far.

Debt

Amount of debt in peso terms, growth levels and proportion to GDP and residual maturities.

Tourism

Visitor arrivals and tourism receipts.

Employment

Employment, unemployment and underemployment rates.

Overseas Filipinos

Deployment and remittances data.
